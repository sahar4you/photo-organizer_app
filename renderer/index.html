Quality Score Guide

0–10 (Poor):
Low quality or thumbnails (usually under ~0.5 MP). Suitable for small previews only.

10–25 (Basic):
Basic quality (~0.5–1 MP). Suitable for social media sharing.

25–50 (Good):
Good quality (~1–3 MP). Suitable for regular viewing and sharing.

50–75 (Better):
Better quality (~3–8 MP). Suitable for large screens or light printing.

75–100 (Best):
Best quality (8+ MP). High sharpness, high resolution, best for printing.

Note: Quality score depends on sharpness, resolution, and file size. Low resolution images may receive lower scores even if they look visually good.
